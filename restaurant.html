about local eateries

every sweet treat found in local, family-owned restaurants, coffee shops, and bakeries is stuffed with a whole lot of love and care. local eateries also frequently get their fresh produce and ingredients from local farmers, so communities thrive on these small bites! small bakeries make use of timeless family recipies to make unique additions to classic dishes. local coffee shops all brew and prepare coffee in a particular way, ensuring a unique experience each time you grab a morning cup of joe. small eateries care about what's on your plate.

why should we support local eateries?

small restaurants and cafés have a lot to offer. in addition to a personalized experience with every purchase, you can be sure that your food is being appropriately sourced since meals are not mass-produced and are made fresh from the kitchen. you'll feel pleased knowing that the food you're eating is healthy and safe for consumption, and you'll make a local entreprenuer's day!
